Hello, I'm

Wisdom Tochi Kalu

A full stack developer, UI/UX enthusiast and a Junior Software Engineer

I am a Software Engineer and Master’s student in Software Engineering for the Green Deal, with a passion for developing sustainable, energy-efficient software solutions. I graduated in 2022 with a first class in Computer Science from my undergraduate university. My journey in technology started with full-stack development and UI/UX design, and over the years, I have expanded my expertise to focus on building optimized, scalable, and high-performance applications that align with sustainability principles.

Currently, I am working on my Masters thesis at a company in Finland, where I am developing a sustainability handbook for developers and a framework for measuring software energy efficiency. This work aligns with my broader mission of reducing the environmental impact of digital solutions by integrating energy-efficient coding practices into modern software development.

Beyond my academic and professional work, I am the founder of The Code4All Initiative, a project dedicated to providing coding and digital literacy skills to underserved communities. Under my leadership, the initiative has grown into a multinational effort, securing over $10,000 in grants and empowering 100+ students with essential tech skills.

My technical skill set include robust knowledge of Java, Kotlin, WASM, PHP, Python, JavaScript, and REST API development, along with expertise in software sustainability methodologies. I am also advanced in my knowledge of OOP concepts and their applications. For example, I designed entirely the website https://myscholarshipinfo.org (now defunct) using OOP.

With a deep-rooted passion for technology’s potential to drive positive change, I am always looking for new challenges that allow me to build innovative and impactful solutions.
